Why PAEK Thermoplastic?

Traditional carbon fiber uses epoxy resin — once cured, it's landfill forever. PAEK (PEEK/PEKK family) is different: it's a high-performance thermoplastic that can be re-melted and reformed. Lighter than aluminum, tougher than thermoset carbon, and fully recyclable.

EU Circular Economy Ready

The EU Circular Economy Action Plan mandates recycled content in all vehicles — including bicycles — starting 2026. PAEK thermoplastic carbon fiber is the only thru axle technology that meets these requirements today.
